We are dedicated to spreading awareness about carbon footprints and their impact on the environment.

Carbon Footprint Formula

Carbon Footprint = Energy Consumed (kWh) × Emission Factor (kg CO₂/kWh)

Example: If you consume **100 kWh** of electricity and the emission factor is **0.92 kg CO₂/kWh**, your carbon footprint is **92 kg CO₂**.

Did you know? The average carbon footprint per person globally is about **4 tons of CO₂ per year**.

Reducing energy consumption by switching to **renewable energy sources** can significantly lower carbon footprints.

Simple actions like **turning off lights**, **unplugging unused devices**, and **using energy-efficient appliances** help reduce carbon emissions.

Carbon footprint is calculated based on energy usage, transportation, and industrial activities.

Burning **1 gallon of gasoline** releases approximately **8.89 kg of CO₂**.

Producing **1 kg of beef** emits around **27 kg of CO₂**, significantly higher than plant-based foods.

Average household energy consumption contributes to **carbon footprint through electricity and heating fuel usage**.

One **hour of flight** per passenger emits approximately **90 kg of CO₂**.

Driving a **car for 1 km** emits about **0.12 kg of CO₂**, depending on fuel efficiency.

Coal-fired power plants release approximately **2.2 pounds of CO₂ per kWh** of electricity generated.

Using an **LED bulb instead of an incandescent** can reduce CO₂ emissions by up to **80%**.

Manufacturing **plastic** contributes to CO₂ emissions, with **1 kg of plastic producing 6 kg of CO₂**.

Public transportation emits **30% less CO₂ per passenger mile** compared to single-occupancy vehicles.

Producing **1 liter of bottled water** emits approximately **0.5 kg of CO₂**.

Using **bicycles instead of cars** can reduce individual transportation-related emissions by up to **75%**.

Recycling **1 ton of paper** saves around **17 trees and reduces CO₂ emissions**.

Shifting to **solar or wind energy** can significantly cut household carbon footprints by **50% or more**.
